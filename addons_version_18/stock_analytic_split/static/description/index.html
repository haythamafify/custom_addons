📦 Stock Analytic Distribution

Bring the power of analytic accounting directly to your stock transfers. Split costs across multiple analytic accounts — the same way you do on invoices.

Key Features

🎯 Analytic Widget on Move Lines
The native analytic_distribution widget appears on every product line in your transfer form — exactly like invoice lines.
✅ Auto Analytic Lines on Validate
When you validate a transfer, analytic lines are automatically created for each distribution percentage on every done move.
🔗 Full Stock Traceability
Analytic lines are linked back to stock.move and stock.picking for full operational traceability.
🌍 Arabic Translation Included
Full Arabic i18n/ar.po translation ships out of the box for Middle East deployments.

How It Works

  1. Open a Transfer — Go to Inventory > Operations > Transfers.
  2. Set Analytic Distribution — On each product line, use the Analytic column to assign accounts and percentages.
  3. Validate — Click Validate. Analytic lines are created automatically per distribution.
  4. Analyze — Review cost allocation in your Analytic Accounting reports.

Technical Notes

  • Distribution stored as JSON in stock.move.analytic_distribution.
  • Cost source uses stock_valuation_layer_ids.value when available; falls back to standard_price × qty.
  • Duplicate distribution keys are skipped to prevent duplicate analytic lines.
  • Multi-plan analytic splits fully supported.
